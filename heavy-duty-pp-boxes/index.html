What Makes a PP Box Heavy Duty

Heavy duty PP corrugated boxes use double-wall or triple-wall flute construction:

  • Double-wall: 8 mm — 880 lb dynamic / 2,200 lb static
  • Triple-wall: 12 mm — 1,320 lb dynamic / 3,300 lb static
  • Corner reinforcements: extruded PP angle inserts
  • Pallet integration: molded-in forklift pockets
